**Topology Summary**

Use a fully differential single-stage NMOS-input telescopic cascode opamp powered from `VDD = 5 V`. The two outputs are high-impedance internal gain nodes: `Voutp` and `Vout`. The structure is symmetric: each side has an NMOS input device, an NMOS cascode above it, a PMOS cascode load, and a PMOS current-source load to `VDD`. A single NMOS tail current source biases both input branches.

**Device/Function List**

- `M5`: NMOS tail current source. Drain at tail node, source/bulk at `0`, gate at `Vbias1`.
- `M1`: NMOS input transistor for `Vinp`. Source at tail node, drain at `nleft`, gate at `Vinp`, bulk tied to source.
- `M2`: NMOS input transistor for `Vinn`. Source at tail node, drain at `nright`, gate at `Vinn`, bulk tied to source.
- `M6`: NMOS cascode above `M1`. Source at `nleft`, drain at `Voutp`, gate at `Vbias2`, bulk tied to source.
- `M7`: NMOS cascode above `M2`. Source at `nright`, drain at `Vout`, gate at `Vbias2`, bulk tied to source.
- `M3`: PMOS upper current-source load on `Voutp` side. Source/bulk at `VDD`, drain at `pleft`, gate at `Vbias4`.
- `M4`: PMOS upper current-source load on `Vout` side. Source/bulk at `VDD`, drain at `pright`, gate at `Vbias4`.
- `M8`: PMOS cascode load on `Voutp` side. Source/bulk at `pleft`, drain at `Voutp`, gate at `Vbias3`.
- `M9`: PMOS cascode load on `Vout` side. Source/bulk at `pright`, drain at `Vout`, gate at `Vbias3`.

**Bias/Reference Strategy**

Use explicit independent DC voltage sources for all named input and bias nodes:

- `Vinp = 2.5 V`
- `Vinn = 2.5 V`
- `Vbias1 = 1.2 V`
- `Vbias2 = 3.0 V`
- `Vbias3 = 2.0 V`
- `Vbias4 = 4.0 V`
- `VDD = 5 V`

These biases are intended to turn on all stacked devices at `.op`, keep the NMOS input pair and cascodes in saturation, and leave enough output headroom for the PMOS cascode loads. Use the requested nominal MOS models: NMOS `VTO=0.7`, `KP=100u`, `W=50u`, `L=1u`; PMOS `VTO=-0.7`, `KP=50u`, `W=100u`, `L=1u`.

**Signal Or Feedback Path**

There is no explicit feedback network. This is an open-loop telescopic gain stage.

- Differential input: `Vinp - Vinn`
- Positive-side output node: `Voutp`
- Negative-side output node: `Vout`

For balanced DC input, both branches should conduct similar current and `Voutp`/`Vout` should settle close to each other. A small increase at `Vinp` increases current in the `Voutp` branch, tending to pull `Voutp` downward relative to `Vout`.

**SPICE/Netlist Constraints**

- Final filename must be `p21.cir`.
- Preserve exact node names: `Vinp`, `Vinn`, `Vbias1`, `Vbias2`, `Vbias3`, `Vbias4`, `Voutp`, `Vout`.
- Include `.op`.
- Do not use subcircuits.
- Do not hide required ports behind renamed aliases.
- Every named input/bias node must be driven by an explicit independent voltage source.
- `Voutp` and `Vout` must remain high-impedance gain nodes between NMOS cascodes and PMOS cascodes.
- Bulk terminals should be tied to each MOSFET source per task guidance.

**Failure Risks To Avoid**

- Do not short `Voutp` and `Vout`; they must be separate differential outputs.
- Do not connect PMOS cascode drains/sources backward; upper PMOS sources go toward `VDD`, cascode drains go to outputs.
- Do not omit DC sources for `Vbias1` through `Vbias4`, `Vinp`, or `Vinn`.
- Do not tie outputs directly to voltage sources or resistive loads that destroy high output impedance.
- Avoid bias choices that cut off any cascode or current-source device at `.op`.
- Avoid using only two NMOS and two PMOS devices; the required topology needs nine MOSFETs total: four NMOS signal/cascode devices, four PMOS load/cascode devices, and one NMOS tail source.
